# LFG Joined Announce Addon

This addon enhances your Mythic+ and group experience in World of Warcraft with two powerful features.

---

## Features

### 1. Group Join Notification

When you join a group via the Group Finder (LFG), a notification frame will appear showing the details of the group you’ve joined (e.g., dungeon name and more).

- If it’s a **Mythic+ group**, a special interactive frame will pop up.
- You can **click this frame** to instantly teleport to the dungeon instance—no need to manually travel!

### 2. Auto Insert Keystone & Leader Tools

When you open the Mythic+ keystone slot interface:

- The addon will **automatically insert your correct keystone** into the slot.
- Two additional buttons appear below the keystone slot:
  - **COUNTDOWN**: Starts a group countdown (only usable by the group leader).
  - **CRF_READY_CHECK**: Sends a ready check to all group members (only usable by the group leader).

> ⚠️ **Note**: If you are **not the group leader**, these two buttons will be disabled.

## 功能说明（中文）

本插件为《魔兽世界》的史诗钥石地下城（Mythic+）和组队体验提供两大实用功能。

### 1. 加入队伍通知

当你通过“寻找队伍”（LFG）系统加入一个队伍时，屏幕上会弹出一个通知框，显示你所加入队伍的详细信息（例如：副本名称和其他）。

- 如果加入的是 **史诗钥石队伍（Mythic+）**，将弹出一个**特殊交互窗口**。
- 你可以**直接点击该窗口**，立即传送至对应副本入口，无需手动跑图！

### 2. 自动插入钥石 + 队长工具

当你打开钥石插槽界面时：

- 插件会**自动将你背包中正确的钥石插入槽位**。
- 界面下方还会显示两个额外按钮：
  - **倒计时（COUNTDOWN）**：为全队启动准备倒计时（仅队长可用）。
  - **就绪确认（CRF_READY_CHECK）**：向全队发送就绪检查（仅队长可用）。

> ⚠️ **注意**：如果你**不是队长**，这两个按钮将处于禁用状态。
